FIL.BORN BUILDERS

Est 2015

Our Story

Fil.Born Builders is a General Building Contractor that offer services in civil-structural, architectural, electro-mechanical and fit-out works for residential, commercial, and industrial buildings.

Our focus is to satisfy our client in providing them good quality, reliability, on time schedule and safety of works in accordance with the company standards and of clients expectation on their construction needs.

The management has a high desire to effectively and efficiently accomplish its goal to constantly seek methods and strategies to ensure that all employees are fully equip and has a full understanding of the importance of quality system.

Who We Are

FIL.BORN BUILDERS is a building Contractor registered with the Department of Trade and Industry. founded on sound professionalism, resourcefulness, honest and trustworthy construction firm combining core professional in the field of Civil Engineering works, Structural Engineering Works, Architectural, Electrical-Mechanical, Roofing and Fit out Works. Among the most significant characteristic of FIL.BORN BUILDERS company policy are personalized service, systematic planning. time-efficient and cost-saving construction methodology. safe working conditions. and healthy working relationship; all equipment towards producing high quality projects and serving satisfied clients.

Our Mission

BUILDERS has a high desire to effectively and efficiently accomplish its goal to constantly seek methods and strategies to ensure that all employees are fully equip and has a full understanding of the importance of quality system.

We are dedicated to accomplish large scale project systematically and promise to make sure the safety, security and welfare of our employees and company properties by promoting health and providing safety program, to achieve zero damages.

Our Vision

Together with our highly motivated employees, we want to cultivate long term relationships with our clients. With these as part of our policy, we provide them the highest quality products and services, proper coordination and communication, warranty, post completion services and maintenance.

We are confident to continue upgrading our procedure and execute development in terms of methodology by means of formulating new techniques and strategies that help to thwart the occurrence of situation that may affect our productivity and effectivity.

Our Core Value

FIL.BORN

FAIRNESS

We value honesty and forthrightness in all our dealings. we take high regard our associates' rights to be fair labor practices. We show objectivity and impartiality in sorting out conflicts

INTEGRITY

Truthfulness is the essential quality of our relationship. By being true and transparent, we understand the concept of trustworthiness. We are ethical in our way and dealing, a trait being valued by our clients.

LIABLE

We take responsibility for ourselves and our staff and don't back away being accountable for our actions. If errors and oversights occur, we addresses and resolve them promptly and effectively.

BE SAFE

Safety is a paramount. We ensure that all workers have undertaken safety training required, and demand workplaces that utilize our people, meet workplace safety standards.

OBSERVANT

We care for the people we work, our customers, our suppliers, and our associates. We show kindness and compassion to our people by ensuring their safety and help them achieve their goals. We protect the planet we live by complying with all the environmental laws and regulations of the land.

RESPECT

Respect for our staff, clients, and suppliers has lead to the foundation of a closely knitted team that takes pride in their work and goes the extra distance to ensure everything runs smoothly and efficiently.

NETWORK AND RESPECT

We create an effective network/system that help our goal in committing work quality. Alongside with these, we understand the foundation of construction is teamwork. Our projects are made with the collective effort of many people working for one purpose in on direction. It is a platform the company built on.
